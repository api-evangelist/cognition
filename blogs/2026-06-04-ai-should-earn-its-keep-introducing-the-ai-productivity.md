Cognition introduced a financial guarantee backed by a computational system that measures Devin's productive engineering contributions: if Devin delivers less engineering value than customers pay for, Cognition will fund usage up to $10M until it does. The announcement shifts the industry focus from activity-based metrics like token consumption toward concrete engineering outcomes, with values validated against engineers' own estimates of comparable work.
